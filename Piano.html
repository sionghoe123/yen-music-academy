🎹 Individual Piano Class Overview

Our one-to-one piano lessons are customized to suit each student's goals — whether for exams, performance, or personal enjoyment. Our experienced instructors provide structured yet fun lessons suitable for all ages, from young children to adults.

👫 Group Piano Class Overview

Our group classes are perfect for beginners to explore piano basics in a collaborative environment. Students learn together, share progress, and motivate each other in a fun, social setting.

  • ✅ Ideal for beginners aged 5 and above
  • ✅ Small group size (2–4 students)
  • ✅ Learn rhythm, notation, and group performance
  • ✅ Affordable and structured introduction to music
